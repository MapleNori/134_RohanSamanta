Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India's civilian space and military missile programs. He was also a beloved teacher, author, and inspirational speaker.Achievements

  • Led India's nuclear tests in 1998.
  • President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
  • Authored several inspirational books like "Wings of Fire".

Quotes

  • "Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
  • "Don't take rest after your first victory because if you fail in second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck."
